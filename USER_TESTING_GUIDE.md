# Moto Vitals - User Testing Guide

Welcome to the User Testing phase of Moto Vitals! This guide will walk you through the steps to test the application and provide feedback for the live staging phase.

## Installation

### Android
1. Download the `app-release.apk` (or the staging version provided).
2. Open the file on your Android device.
3. If prompted, allow installation from unknown sources.
4. Open Moto Vitals.

### iOS (TestFlight)
1. Install the **TestFlight** app from the App Store.
2. Open the invitation link sent to your email.
3. Tap **Accept** and then **Install**.

## Test Scenarios

### 1. Onboarding & Registration
- **Goal**: Successfully create an account and set up your first vehicle.
- **Steps**:
    1. Open the app and tap **Sign Up**.
    2. Enter your email and password.
    3. Choose your vehicle model (e.g., Honda Winner X).
    4. Enter your current odometer reading.
- **Success Criteria**: You should see your vehicle dashboard with initial maintenance health.

### 2. Dashboard & Maintenance Health
- **Goal**: View the health of your motorcycle parts.
- **Steps**:
    1. Observe the "Health Rings" on the dashboard.
    2. Tap on a part (e.g., Engine Oil) to see details.
- **Success Criteria**: The health percentages should reflect your current odometer reading.

### 3. Logbook (Service Logs)
- **Goal**: Record a maintenance activity.
- **Steps**:
    1. Tap the **+** button on the dashboard or go to the **Logbook** tab.
    2. Fill in the service details: Part (e.g., Chain Lubrication), Odometer, and Date.
    3. Tap **Save**.
- **Success Criteria**: The log should appear in the Digital Logbook, and the part's health ring should reset to 100%.

### 4. Community Gallery
- **Goal**: Browse and interact with build posts.
- **Steps**:
    1. Go to the **Gallery** tab.
    2. Scroll through the posts.
    3. Tap the **Like** icon on a post.
- **Success Criteria**: The like count should increment, and you should see the post details.

### 5. Marketplace
- **Goal**: Find parts for your motorcycle.
- **Steps**:
    1. Go to the **Marketplace** tab.
    2. Browse the listed parts.
    3. Tap on a part to view it in the browser (affiliate link).
- **Success Criteria**: The app should open the external link successfully.

## Reporting Bugs & Feedback

If you encounter any issues or have suggestions, please report them using the following methods:
- **Email**: support@motovitals.ph
- **Google Form**: [Link to Feedback Form]
- **Discord**: #beta-testing channel

### What to include in a bug report:
- **Device**: (e.g., Samsung S21, iPhone 13)
- **OS Version**: (e.g., Android 13, iOS 16)
- **Steps to reproduce**: How did you find the bug?
- **Expected behavior**: What should have happened?
- **Actual behavior**: What actually happened?
- **Screenshots/Videos**: If possible.
